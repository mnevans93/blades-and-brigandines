BLADES AND BRIGANDINES

Welcome to Blades and Brigandines. In this roguelike game, you will step into the sandals of a gladiator fighting their way to fame and glory within the storied Arena. As your foes fall before you, you will gain experience and grow ever stronger, and the spoils of your victories may be spent on new weapons and armor to enhance your combat prowess. Many come to the Arena, but few leave with their lives. What does your fate have in store for you?

Type in your gladiator's name below and Enter the Arena to begin, or click How to Play for an explanation of basic game mechanics.


How To Play


Getting Started

Your gladiator has six primary stats. As your gladiator gains experience and levels up, you gain additional stat points to further increase their power.
Strength - Determines how much damage your gladiator deals in addition to base weapon damage. Also increases Energy required for your attacks
Attack - Determines how accurate your gladiator's attacks are. Contested by your enemy's Defense stat
Defense - Determines how hard your gladiator is to hit. Contested by your enemy's Attack stat
Vitality - Determines how much Health your gladiator has
Stamina - Determines how much Energy your gladiator has for attacking, defending, taunting, or winning the crowd
Charisma - Determines bonus gold earned from fights, shop discounts, and the effectiveness of Taunts and Win the Crowd
Additionally, your gladiator has equipment to aid them in battle in the form of weapons and armor. Weapons increase your damage potential while armor makes you more durable and adds to your Armor pool.


Combat Basics

Combat is turn-based. Each gladiator has the following actions available to them:
Attacks (light, medium, heavy) - The gladiator attacks with their weapon. The heavier the attack, the more damage it will deal, the less accuracy it will have, the higher the chance to land a critical strike, and the higher the Energy consumption
Defend - The gladiator uses their turn to defend, becoming harder to hit while also protecting themselves from critical strikes until their next turn
Rest - The gladiator uses their turn to rest and regain 50% of their Energy pool
Taunt - The gladiator uses their turn to try and rile up their opponent. If successful, the target receives a random debuff, or they miss their turn as the taunter goads them into attacking before being countered for a free light hit
Win the Crowd - The gladiator uses their turn to excite the crowd. The higher the mood of the crowd, the more gold that will be rewarded for the fight.


The Arena

While in the arena, you have multiple options available to you for taking on your next opponent. First you must select whether it is a fight to the death or a fight until either gladiator yields. Fights to the death offer substantially higher gold and experience rewards than fights ending in yields, but beware: if your gladiator loses their life in the arena, they are dead for good.
Additionally, you may select a normal, hard, or deadly fight. The harder the fight, the more powerful your opponent, but the greater the rewards for victory.


The Town

The town is the hub in which you will spend all of your time while outside of the Arena. Aside from your room in the inn for resting after your fights, you can find both a weaponsmith's shop and an armorsmith's shop.
